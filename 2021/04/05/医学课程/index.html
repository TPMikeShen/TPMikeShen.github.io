Outline of Program Study—Medicine course

Lesson 1

  1. The 4 key check points

    UCAS Application (on-line form)

​ UKCAT (dynamic knowledge) some universities need

​ BMAT (more medical & essay) some universities need

​ Final interviews (most difficult)

  1. Choose for 4 medical schools

    Oxford or Cambridge (not both)

​ Avoid choosing more than 2 BMAT universities when you don’t know your score

​ Avoid schools which offer different course structures

​ Avoid applying for all

  1. Course considerations

    Location (London is expensive to live in)

    Types of schools

    City (travel an hour to lectures & big)

    Campus (easy to access & small)

    Collegiate (different disciplines)

  2. Course structure

    Traditional

    Lectures, tutorials, particular topics, homework weekly (first 2-3 years), lab, purely-science-based, pre-clinical year

    Integrated

    Taught by topics

    Problem based learning (less common these years)

    Group work

  3. The personal statement

    First point, 4000 characters, a core component, must stand out

    tip: start & end are crucial

    ​ enthusiasm is also important

5.1 Key structure

Write about the course you choose-why traditional course or something else

Work experience-reflect on what you learned

Skills and achievements-how related to a excellent medical student

Hobbies and interests-evidence for skills gained

Future plans-what area of medicine interests you

5.2 Skills and achievements

​ key skills examples

communication skills Duke of Edinburgh Award

​ leader ship Young enterprise

5.3 Work experience (essential component)

Hospital setting, GP, International, First aid, Volunteering, Charity work

  1. Timetable for exams

    UKCAT-1. July-5. October (27 medical schools need)

    BMAT-2. November (7 medical schools need) (2h pen and paper test)

Lesson 2

  1. How to answer a scientific question

    Analyses the question in your mind-20s

    Structure your answer-20s

    Actually answer the question verbally-10s

  2. Four kinds of main subjects

    Biology/Biological Science Biomedical Science Biochemistry Natural Science
    admission tests no BMAT no NSAA
    study years 3 3 3/4 3
    features interesting medicine pure & more subjects (especially chemistry) focus on all the sciences
    future jobs ecosystem researcher doctor scientist scientist
