文书段落写作3

Posted on 2021-08-14

Describe an accomplishment, event, or realization that sparked a period of personal growth and a new understanding of yourself or others. (Maximum 350 words)

In 2020, shortly after my IGCE exams, teachers and parents cooperated to organize a professional experience in the workplace, which let my classmates and I find our interests in future careers such as banking, network technology researcher, medicine and learn more about the work content in detail.

There were eight choices and these included Agricultural Bank of China, Network Technology Company, work experience in a dental hospital and so on. I was interested in a job related to biology and health, moreover I dreamt of becoming a doctor, so I chose the dental hospital placement.

Dentists use a lot of new technology and one area we studied was digital scanning which is used for oral modeling. The scanner transmitted the real-time oral image to the computer which automatically generated the tooth model immediately. As the dentist manipulated the scanner in her hand, she adjusted the image on the computer to make it complete. I realized that nowadays, almost every job uses computer technology which is something I learnt on this work placement. Then, training was given in plaque staining and scientific brushing. After rinsing with water, red edible dye showed teeth with soft debris. When the leading dentist worked with a student to give a demonstration of how to brush teeth in the right way, she did every step carefully in order to avoid any mistakes and was very thorough in her instructions which was helpful for learning quickly. Compared with doing surgical operations, this is simpler and less stressful. A dentist needs to communicate with his patients patiently to make sure everything goes well. I discovered that a clear mind is also very important for most activities in life! Additionally, a calm mind can ensure that timely remedial measures will quickly come to mind and avoid irreparable consequences, such as sequelae of the patient.

I benefited a lot from this visit and discovered that I am suitable for this kind of work which requires patience and care. Furthermore, I need to put more effort into the study of computer programming since it appears to be very important in this field.

​ SHEN ZIYOU
