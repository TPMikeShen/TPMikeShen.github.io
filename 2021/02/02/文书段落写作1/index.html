文书段落写作1

Posted on 2021-02-02

My name is Shen Ziyou and my English name is Mike. I am a 16-year-old student in Jinglin High School and hoping to go to university to study biochemistry in when I pass my exams in 2022. I have a big family, but I live with my parents in Nanjing. Furthermore, I have many hobbies, such as playing the piano, playing badminton and listening to music. Moreover, in my free time, I like reading books too, in particular, science fiction. Sometimes, I also read some classic works and literature novels such as Journey to the West.

In my mother’s eyes, I am not an extrovert or an active boy before other people, but I always focus on what I am doing and I am not talkative. I can do things well because of my patience and carefulness. I am very persistent and determined. For instance, I have been practicing playing the piano and devoting most of my time to it for almost twelve years. As a result, I have a useful skill which someone else may not have and I believe that it will help my life in some way one day. On the other hand, in my friends’ opinions, maybe I am shy and seldom talk to others, but I get on well with people I know well and can trust.

I am very proud of an activity I took part in at Primary School when I was a child. In the beginning, my classmates and I were put into groups and asked to pick up rubbish on the streets. Around our school, there were so much garbage, such as plastic bottles, cigarette butts and packing paper. I had thought that it would not take us much time to clean several streets, however, unexpectedly, we were doing this the whole morning until 12 o’clock that day. Because of this, we were all tired out and wanted to lie on the ground at once when we finished! Through this activity, I not only learnt that this is not easy work, but also realized the importance of protecting the environment. Most importantly, it was the first time that I did something to help society with my own hands.

In conclusion, this was an unforgettable experience because it was meaningful and Inspiring. This taught me excellent skills for my future such as teamworking and improving my communication skills.
