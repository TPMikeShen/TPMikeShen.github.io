文书段落写作6

Posted on 2022-02-28

Think about an academic subject that inspires you. Describe how you have furthered this interest in and/or out of the classroom. (Maximum 350 words）

Biology is the subject which has inspired me the most and I have been interested in this subject since I first learnt it in my junior high school.

Unfortunately, it was not part of my senior High School curriculum, so I decided to do further studies outside the school. My biology course started in a training institution which was a study center of AQA (The Assessment and Qualifications Alliance) and this was the first time I began my interest in this particular subject. During class, I learned about cell structure, human body mechanisms and so on. Apart from focusing on the topics in the textbook, I also discussed derived questions such as why COVID-19 mutates so rapidly with my teacher after class since she was keen on connecting theory with real life.

However, my extracurricular activities were more than that. I read many books in this field such as “The Selfish Gene” (Richard Dawkins, 1976) which was my favorite. From the book, I not only expanded my breadth of knowledge, but also learnt some of the author’s opinions such as “Even if we are all driven by selfish genes, good people will be rewarded” which has developed my critical thinking.

In addition, I went to a biopharmaceutical company and took part in several experiments. These improved my practical skills by learning how to use different experimental apparatus such as a pipette gun in the laboratory. Furthermore, I was invited to join in a project in the company about a new biomedical drug invention. Although I could only take part in a small section of the whole process, I was very pleased to do it since this was a perfect chance for me to see how professional staff can be involved in the production of a new drug which would benefit people suffering from gastroenteritis, which was closely connected to real life.

To summarize, the experience improved my ability to solve problems and come up with new ideas to solve real life dilemmas as well as learning basic knowledge about this career. This will lay the foundation for my broader career choice in the future.
