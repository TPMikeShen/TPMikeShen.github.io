病理AI公司参观

Posted on 2022-01-27

Today, I wnet to a compamy called 91360 which aims to provide AI technology for assisting pathologic diagnosis, which is called cloud diagnostics and remote consultation of medical care, but I wnat to focus on the first part. This project is TB which means it is towards business which are hospitals.

The company had gotten the first China class III medical device certificate from FDA. It was really hard due to the difficulty of entering this career and scarcity of workers in the specific filed who are called pathologists with at least 10 years’ working experience to be experts. Although they do not contact with patients directly, they need to have the ability to dignose diseases such as what kinds of cancer the patient had by observing cell or tissue sections under light microscope. There is a huge workload since all kinds of cancer cells are different in structures and the number of cells within a small area is so large. However, this technology combines the AI with the dignosing process by converting the physical section of cells into digital image. The algorithm can use the microscpoe and scanner which together form slice scanner inside the system to mark the positions of abnormal cells in the whole area contaning about twenty thousand cells. This offers a easier way for pathologists to screen the cancer cells and make further decisions.

There are two main parts for the promotions of innovation. One is cytology and the other is histology. The main difference between them is use of detathced cells and a piece of tissue though they are both one thin layer of cells. Additionally, the difference also contains traumatic and atraumatic. It needs to be mentioned that the second method offers a more precise relative position of the tumor.

Apart from the basic information about the mechanism, the technology can not completly replace all pathologists yet as some experts are necessary to face new situations or problems that the computer can not solve. Also, the avalible sample size for the study of AI is small and the accuracy can only get 98% by learning at least 1 million samples. Moreover the knowledge beyond the present cognition of people can not be learned by the AI. In this way, AI is not so good as human brain in innovation although it has better abilities in processing data, integrated presentation and other basic work. Furthermore, the existed laws can not deal with the damage of people caused by AI which means it has some ethical problems.

Finally, the most important thing I learned from the teacher today is the language study. In short, it can help people in many aspects including expression, imgination, analysis, observation and coming up with new idea as well as solution beyond the science Learning.
